Merge related verticals.

Restaurants need a unique set of tools to run effectively. Historically, these tools have been isolated products that the restaurant operator has to pay for and manage individually.

  • POS (per terminal per month) ~$50
  • Time & Attendance (pm) ~$100
  • Payroll (pm) ~$30
  • Scheduling (pm) ~$100


Smooth integrates these verticals into a single platform, at a flat rate of $99 a month, no device limits.

Smashing cost barriers.

By re-thinking the architecture for restaurant technology, we've reduced the typical hardware footprint and cost by an order of magnitude.

  • Industry avg. setup $15,000
  • Smooth Setup $1,500

We see our approach to the market as 3 distinct phases...

Phase 1

Re-define the restaurant hardware & software landscape by driving up the value of the software offering while simultaneously driving down hardware and software costs.

How do we do that?

  1. By creating new innovative features that restaurateurs and their teams will love.
  2. By improving [by an order of magnitude] the efficiency and UX of features that most POS apps already provide.
  3. By aggregating features from various vertical markets that belong together but are currently not offered together.
  4. By doing away with old school clunky client-server architecture in favor of smarter modern architecture.

Phase 2



Our starting point was to take a big leap forward for restaurant software in terms of UX and functionality. But in Phase 2, we want to focus on the really innovative stuff like AI, enhanced customer experience, specialized hardware, etc.

We've prototyped and rolled out some hardware, like our POS and Kitchen devices, as well as our own surveillance cameras (Smoothcam), and an early version of our Smart Shelves. But there's much more to do. We can greatly improve restaurant performance through smart hardware, and we can make innovation affordable to restaurants by using modern architecture and open source.

What follows is a rought outline of what we have in mind...

PHASE 2 /automation

  • center_focus_weak Voice and Vision

    We've been tinkering with voice recognition, speech generation, and computer vision combined with machine learning. The effect of these technologies on the restaurant landscape is going to be absolutely transformative.

  • precision_manufacturing Restaurant AI

    If we give the restaurant a brain, it can manage inventory, do menial prep tasks, smart surveillance, customer-recognition, gesture and mood detection, automated cleaning, atmosphere control, energy management, pre-emptive reporting, and so much more.

    Cash remains a loophole for theft, and is the culprit behind some of the most time-consuming tasks. If we design the hardware and software specifically around restaurant cash handling, we can fully digitize cash management and reporting and shut down the loopholes.

PHASE 2 /transactions

  • credit_card Payment processing

    Smooth mobile payment devices will allow us to take full control of the billing experience in-store to avoid device bottlenecks, speed up check settlement and automate cash-up and accounting tasks. This unlocks our most important revenue stream.

  • account_tree Control Money Flow

    Smooth is already at the heart of the operation. By managing the flow of money from restaurant to suppliers, restaurant to staff, customer to restaurant, etc., we'd be able to do instant clearance between connected parties, provide escrow between buyer and seller, and significantly reduce admin and accounting overheads. This opens another sizable revenue stream for Smooth while simultaneously reducing costs and complexity for our clients.

PHASE 2 /blockchain

  • deblur Decentralized Architecture

    With our own "offline-first" blockchain implementation, we can solve internet dependency problems and lower cloud/server costs by an order of magnitude, in one fell swoop. It's technically still SaaS, but it takes scalability and robustness to a whole new level.

  • emoji_events Crypto-based Rewards Program

    Instead of the same old paper rewards program, we're thinking "the crypto currency of the restaurant business". Customers can earn and spend their coins at restaurants everywhere, or trade them at an exchange for Bitcoin, USDT, or whatever they like.

  • currency_bitcoin On-ramp and Off-ramp

    We provide the on-ramp and off-ramp for restaurants between fiat and crypto, which eliminates the complexity around accepting crypto as payment. At the same time, we provide a means within Smooth (for the bold and brave) to track crypto on the balance sheet without needing your own CA to sort that mess out. We expect this trend to pick up sharply over the next decade and restaurateurs will need a simple way to deal with the related accounting and supply chain problems.

Phase 3

Goal: deeper integration with industry processes to deliver more value, and open new revenue streams.
